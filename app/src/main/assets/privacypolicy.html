    6. HOW WE USE COOKIES
    Cookies are small data files of information that a website or mobile application transfers to your hard drive or mobile device to store and sometimes track information about you. Cookies do identify a user's device but they do not personally identify users. In addition, mobile devices may use other tracking files which are similar to cookies (e.g. iOS devices use Apple's 'identifier for advertisers' (IDFA) and Android devices use Google's Android ID). In the context of tracking within an application, the concept of a cookie will include an IDFA and an Android ID for the purpose of this Privacy Policy. We use cookies to offer, secure and improve our services to you, including by remembering your preferences, recognizing you when you use our mobile application and tailoring ads to your interests. To successfully achieve this, we may also link information from cookies with other personal information we hold about you. When you use our mobile application, some or all of the following types of cookies may be set on your device.

    6.1. Types of cookies we use
    BLINQUI may use the following cookies:
    1. Strictly necessary cookies: these are cookies that are required for the operation of the mobile application, for example to allow you to log into secure areas.
    2. Performance cookies: these types of cookies recognise and count the number of users of our mobile application and to see how users navigate around the application. This information is used to improve the way our mobile application works.
    3. Functionality cookies: these cookies recognise you when you return to the application, enable personalised content and recognise and remember your preferences.
    4. Targeting cookies: these cookies record your visit to the application, including the individual pages visited and the links followed.
